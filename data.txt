In fields where green and yellow meet,  
A game of skill and passion sweet,  
Cricket's roar, a timeless tune,  
Bats swing, and balls fly soon.  

Bowler’s spin, a tricky art,  
Batsman’s courage, beating heart,  
Fields of hope, dreams in flight,  
Under the sun’s warm glow and light.  

From village grounds to grandest stadiums,  
Cricket unites, breaks down walls, and  
In every cheer, in every play,  
The spirit of cricket finds its way.
